BillPal 1.6

What is it?

BillPal allows you easily keep track of your bills by arranging them in a table. Attributes presented are expiration date, amount, reference number, IBAN number, and comment among others. User can reorganise the table by rearranging the columns or sorting by a specific attribute. Also a convenient colour coding lets the user to spot fast the bills that are soon to expire making it easy to keep track of how one is doing.

Development status: minor usability improvements pending.
